Richfield Moodle Support - FAQ

To log in to the Moodle student portal, visit the Richfield Moodle login page and enter your student credentials. If you're logging in for the first time, use the default password provided by the institution and change it upon your first login.

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and you'll receive a link to reset your password. If you face any issues, please contact the support team.

Once you're logged in, you'll see a dashboard with a list of all your enrolled courses. Click on any course to access its content, including lectures, assignments, and quizzes.

If you encounter any technical issues while using Moodle, please reach out to the Richfield Moodle Support team at [support-email@richfield.edu] or call our helpline at [123-456-7890].
